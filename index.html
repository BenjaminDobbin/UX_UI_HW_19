Benjamin Dobbin

My background is in photography, interior design, and people management, but I transitioned into the world of product design when I discovered my passion for combining user research with UX design. Being able to unite my visual storytelling skills with my enthusiasm for problem-solving & team management is what I find most fulfilling. I believe great design demands tackling complex problems with compassion and envisioning innovative solutions.
